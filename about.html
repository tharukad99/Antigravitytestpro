Our Story

From a small kitchen to your heart.

Baking with Passion Since 2015

CakeyNuts began with a simple mission: to create cakes that not only look spectacular but taste even better. What started as a hobby in a small home kitchen has grown into a beloved local bakery, known for our attention to detail and premium ingredients.

We believe that every celebration deserves a centerpiece that sparks joy. That's why we pour our heart and soul into every batter, frosting, and decoration. Whether it's a grand wedding or a quiet afternoon tea, we're here to make it sweeter.
